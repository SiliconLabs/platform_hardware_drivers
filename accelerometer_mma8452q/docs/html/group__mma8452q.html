sl_status_t mma8452q_deinit ( void  )

De-initalize the MMA8452Q driver.

Parameters
[in]i2cspmThe I2C peripheral to use.

De-initialization of the MMA8452Q resets the device settings and places the device in standby mode, disabling the analog subsystem and internal clocks.

Note
The purpose of de-initialization is to shutdown the MMA8452Q as part of a complete shutdown sequence for an EFM32/EFR32-based system.
